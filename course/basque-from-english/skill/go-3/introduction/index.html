Go-3

NORA – allative case

  • Proper nouns
  • ending -(e)ra
  • if the noun ends in a vowel, add -ra
  • Italia - Italiara
  • if the noun ends in a consonant, add -era
  • Irun - Irunera

Lesson 3 Vocabulary

  • Italia - Italy
  • Italiara - To Italy
  • Alemania - Germany
  • Alemaniara - To Germany
  • Irunera - To Irun
  • Euskal Herri - Basque Country
  • Euskal Herrira - to Basque Country
